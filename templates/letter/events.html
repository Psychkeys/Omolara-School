Sports and Games

It is that time of the school year, where we have fun by participating inextracurricular events. We believe that you are as excited about this as we are.

This is another opportunity for our students and kids to have fun and learn at the same time by participating in various extracurricular activities and sports.

Sports for kids and young students is not only fun, it helps to build positive character and develop great attitude

There are so many health benefits of sports as an activity for students. Sports and exercises must be encouraged across all levels of learning. It is important that in as much as students are having fun learning in the classroom, they must engage in outdoor and physical activities to keep them active and happy.

Physical & Mental Health Benefits

Exercise makes kids healthier and happier. Active kids have stronger immune systems, strong cardiovascular, respiratory systems, bone and muscular structure.

Exercise improves blood flow, ensuring that more oxygen reaches the heart and brain, promoting higher energy levels. Activities also makes kids breathe and perspire more efficiently, which helps to naturally remove toxins from the body.

A healthy body leads to a healthy mind - we have lean from studies active kids also enjoy better mental health, including better memory, ability to focus and concentrate for longer hours. More confidence and improved social skills. It helps improve confidence, ability to work well in a team, and develop better social skills. When kids have high self-esteem, they are more likely to be happier, more optimistic and more inclined to assume leadership roles.
