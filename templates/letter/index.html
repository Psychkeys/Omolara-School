Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can we apply for admission?

    You can reach out to us through any of our contacts, stating your requests and enquiry. Someone will be available to guide you.

  • What is Montessori?

    It is a system of education for young children that seeks to develop natural interests and activities rather than use formal teaching methods.

  • Is O.M.S a Montessori School?

    Omolara Montessori School is complaint with the montessori method of teaching as we adopt all five methods of montessori principles.

  • Are there Boarding facilties available?

    Currently, O.M.C is a day school. We are working consistently to make sure we have boarding facilties available for our future students

  • Are you a Religious School?

    No, we accept students from all religious background without discrimination.

  • Why Omolara?

    We believe in the potential within every child just as the name suggests. We are committed to tapping into the unending power within every child for greatness.
